Sites

Sensoji

Sensoji Temple is the oldest and most popular temple in Tokyo. "Kaminari" Gate is known as the symbol of the place. Entering to the temple from the gate, you can meet "Nakamise Dori", which has 89 shops in 250 meters road.

Tsukiji

Tsukiji has the largest scale of fish market in the world. If you want to eat your fill with sushi, it's the best choice to visit there. You can see the tuna auction in the early morning.

Akihabara

Here is heaven of Japanese Anime!! All anime fans must visit here. You should also go to "Maid Cafe" where waitresses dress like maids. Enjoy "Otaku" culture!!
